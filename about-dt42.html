About DT42

The company behind AIKEA

Who We Are

DT42 was founded in 2016. We focus on building lightweight and intelligent edge AI solutions. Our systems can run on mobile phones and IoT devices to allow AI to run without internet connections.

In the 5G era, our solution is the best partner for mobility, smart city, and factory automation. DT42 is an official partner of NVIDIA, Intel, Qualcomm and ARM.

AIKEA is the key product built by DT42 to provide elegant and simple edge AI experience to customers.

Our Edge AI Technology

At DT42, we specialize in edge AI solutions that bring intelligence directly to devices without requiring constant cloud connectivity. This approach offers several key advantages:

Enhanced Privacy

Data stays on your device, not in the cloud

Reduced Latency

Faster response times without network delays

Offline Operation

Functions without internet connectivity

Energy Efficiency

Optimized for lower power consumption

Our lightweight AI models are specifically designed to run efficiently on resource-constrained devices while maintaining high accuracy and performance.
