Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my AIKEA device?

Setting up your AIKEA device is simple. The Full Kit comes pre-configured and ready to use. Just plug it in, connect to your Wi-Fi, and follow the included setup guide to link it with your Telegram account.

How do I change the detection target?

You can change the detection target through the AIKEA configuration interface. The process is detailed in the user manual included with your device.

Is my data secure with AIKEA?

Yes, AIKEA processes all data locally on the device. No video footage is stored in the cloud, ensuring your privacy is protected.

Can I customize the AIKEA software?

Absolutely! AIKEA is 100% open-source, allowing you to customize both the hardware and software to suit your specific needs.

How do I get support for my AIKEA device?

You can reach our support team via email at support@aikea.co or by filling out the contact form on this page.
