About AIKEA

What is AIKEA

AIKEA is a home camera powered by AI which can capture important moments automatically for you. It's designed to be simple, safe, and elegant, making it the perfect addition to any home.

With its compact size of just 5cm x 7cm, AIKEA can be placed anywhere in your home, blending seamlessly with your decor while providing powerful AI-driven monitoring capabilities.

Features & Benefits

SIMPLE & ELEGANT

AIKEA is built with simplicity at its core. The sensor-like appearance and compact shape allow you to place it anywhere at home without disrupting your interior design.

With dimensions of just 5cm x 7cm, AIKEA is small enough to be unobtrusive yet powerful enough to monitor your home effectively.

  • Compact size (5cm x 7cm)
  • Minimalist design
  • Easy to place anywhere

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

AIKEA is created with built-in AI. All intelligent computation is done locally inside AIKEA to protect your privacy. No cloud collection of your home videos means your data stays with you.

In an era where data privacy is increasingly important, AIKEA stands out by processing everything on-device, ensuring your personal moments remain private.

  • On-device AI processing
  • No cloud storage of videos
  • Your data stays in your home
  • Enhanced security and privacy

AIKEA supports detection of 20+ targets including dogs, cats, intruders, cars, babies and bicycles. You may easily switch between targets based on your need.

How AIKEA Works

AIKEA uses advanced AI to monitor your home and notify you of important events. Here's how the process works:

1

Event Detection

AIKEA's built-in AI continuously monitors its field of view. When it detects an event matching your selected target (such as a pet, person, or vehicle), it processes this information locally on the device.

2

Notification

Once an event is detected, AIKEA captures an image of the event and sends it directly to your Telegram account. This notification happens in near real-time, allowing you to stay informed about what's happening at home.

3

Review

Open your Telegram app to view the captured image. You can check what triggered the alert and decide if any action is needed. All of this happens without storing your videos in the cloud, maintaining your privacy.

No cloud collection is involved in this process. Your privacy is protected at every step.
