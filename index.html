What is AIKEA

AIKEA is a home camera powered by AI which can capture important moments automatically for you.

Key Features

SIMPLE & ELEGANT

AIKEA is built with simple concept. The sensor-like appearance and shape allow you to place it anywhere at home.

Dimensions: 5cm x 7cm

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

It is created with built-in AI. All intelligent computation is done locally inside AIKEA to protect your privacy. No cloud collection of your home videos.

MULTIPLE TARGETS

AIKEA supports detection of 20+ targets including dogs, cats, intruders, cars, babies and bicycles. You may easily switch between targets based on your need.

OPEN SOURCE

AIKEA is 100% open-sourced and users may customize AIKEA in the best you prefer, like turning it into a dinosaur. All components are compatible with Raspberry Pi and are reusable in order to reduce the electronic wastes.
