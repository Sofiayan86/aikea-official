How AIKEA Can Help You

AIKEA's AI-powered detection system can identify over 20 different targets, making it versatile for various home monitoring needs. Here are some of the most popular ways people are using AIKEA in their homes.

Intruder Detection

Keep your home secure by monitoring for unexpected visitors. AIKEA can detect when someone enters your property and immediately send you a notification with an image.

How It Works

  1. Place AIKEA in a strategic location with a view of entry points
  2. Set detection target to "person" or "intruder"
  3. Receive instant Telegram notifications when someone is detected
  4. Review the image to determine if action is needed

Benefits

  • Peace of mind when you're away from home
  • Immediate notifications of potential security concerns
  • Visual confirmation of who is at your property
  • No monthly subscription fees like traditional security systems
  • Privacy-focused with no cloud storage of your footage

Cat Toilet Detection

Monitor your cat's litter box habits to ensure they're healthy and using their designated area. AIKEA can detect when your cat uses the litter box and send you a notification.

How It Works

  1. Position AIKEA with a view of your cat's litter box
  2. Set detection target to "cat"
  3. Receive Telegram notifications when your cat uses the litter box
  4. Track your cat's bathroom habits over time

Benefits

  • Monitor your cat's health through bathroom habits
  • Detect potential health issues early through changes in frequency
  • Ensure your cat is using the litter box properly
  • Helpful for multi-cat households to track individual cats
  • Useful for pet sitters to confirm cat care while you're away
